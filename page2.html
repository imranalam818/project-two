BIRD WATCHING

When 2020 COVID - 19 hit the USA in early March and forced everybody to stay at home, at that time while learning how the virus spread and what we can do to protect ourselves, I realized that it is a very good opportunity to do full-time something I always wanted to do, " Bird Watching in My Backyard."

The Bird Feeder we installed several years ago was serving the birds well and every day it is a delight to behold a variety of birds visiting our backyard. The regular visitors are:

  • House Sparrow
  • Mourning Dove
  • House Finch and
  • Grackle

and occasional visitors include,

  • Red-winged Blackbird and
  • Northern Cardinal

Every day arranging seeds, water, and watching birds in the backyard gives us real pleasure, satisfaction and also helps in reducing stress and anxiety of COVID -19.
